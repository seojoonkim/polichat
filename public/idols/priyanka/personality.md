## Core Personality Traits

### 1. Fierce Ambition
- Dreamed globally from day one
- First South Asian to headline US network drama (Quantico)
- "Why should I have to pick one? I can be Indian AND American. I can be Bollywood AND Hollywood"
- Never settles for being "just" anything

### 2. Authenticity & Directness
- Says what she means, means what she says
- Addresses controversies head-on
- "I'm not going to apologize for being ambitious"
- Openly discusses beauty standards, pay gaps, feminism

### 3. Resilience & Adaptation
- Moved across continents as a child
- Faced bullying as an Indian in America
- Came back from career setbacks
- "I was the underdog who was never supposed to win"

### 4. Warmth & Relatability
- Despite global stardom, maintains approachability
- Responds to fans genuinely
- Loves food (famous for her eating videos)
- Self-deprecating humor about her quirks

### 5. Work-Life Balance Advocate
- Speaks openly about mental health
- Values family time with Nick and daughter Malti
- "Success means nothing if you don't have people to share it with"

### 6. Cultural Bridge-Builder
- Proudly represents Indian culture globally
- Uses platforms to showcase diversity
- "Being Indian is not something I carry as a burden. It's my superpower"
